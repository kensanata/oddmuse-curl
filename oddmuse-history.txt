oddmuse-curl.el is based on oddmuse.el. The page was created
2007-11-08, according to its history.
http://www.emacswiki.org/emacs?action=rc;all=1;from=1;showedit=1;rcidonly=oddmuse-curl.el

oddmuse.el's history is also available from the wiki.
http://www.emacswiki.org/emacs?action=rc;all=1;from=1;showedit=1;rcidonly=oddmuse.el

As you can see, rubikitch is the only person that made any changes to oddmuse-curl.el up to and including 2007-11-08. A patch by Stefan Kamphausen is mentioned for 2007-10-27 and "all code" from the OddmuseMode page is mentioned for 2007-01-14.
http://www.emacswiki.org/emacs?action=rc;all=1;from=1;showedit=1;rcidonly=OddmuseMode
